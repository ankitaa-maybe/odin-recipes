Veg Waffle

Here's the recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soft butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 cup self rising flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • a pinch of baking soda
  • 1 tsp lemon juice butter for greasing
For Serving honey maple syrup chocolate sauce Method
  1. Combine the butter, sugar, milk and vanilla essence in a deep bowl and mix well using a whisk.
  2. Add the self-rising flour, salt and baking soda, mix well using a whisk.
  3. Add the lemon juice and mix well.
  4. Pre-heat a waffle iron.
  5. Grease the waffle iron with little oil, use 1/3 of the batter to spread it evenly into 2 waffle moulds. Put some oil over the batter and cook in a waffle maker for 8 to 9 minutes or till the waffles are crispy.
  6. Repeat for the remaining batter to make 4 more waffles.
  7. Serve immediately with honey, maple syrup or chocolate sauce.
